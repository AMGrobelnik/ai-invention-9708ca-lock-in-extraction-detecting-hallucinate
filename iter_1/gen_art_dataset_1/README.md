## Full Summary

Two-dataset benchmark for lock-in hallucination coefficient experiments. PRIMARY: FActScore-ChatGPT (157 biographies, 183 total — 26 filtered for <5 atoms) with per-atom binary labels (S=1/NS=0, IR skipped), enabling direct lock-in coefficient calculation via Hadamard variant documents. SECONDARY: SummaCoz train+val (6069 article/summary pairs, label 0=inconsistent, label 2=consistent) with NL explanations of inconsistencies. FRANK discarded (0/2246 faithful — error-taxonomy only, not a balanced benchmark). XSumFaith discarded (no source article text, 87% imbalanced). Total: 6226 examples across 2 datasets.
